Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

Categories

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), as well as PayPal and Apple Pay. For enterprise customers, we also support wire transfers. All transactions are processed securely with 256-bit encryption.

How can I upgrade my plan?

You can upgrade your plan at any time by going to the "Account Settings" page and selecting "Change Plan." Choose your desired plan and confirm the changes. Any prorated charges or credits will be automatically calculated.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! We have native apps for both iOS and Android devices. You can find them in the App Store and Google Play Store by searching for our product name. The mobile app offers all the core functionality of the web version.
